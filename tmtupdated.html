Our Story

ThingyMagicky Treats began as a passion project in a small Vanderbijlpark kitchen, with a simple mission: to create treats that bring joy and connection to every occasion.

We believe in baking with love, using only the freshest ingredients, and putting our heart into every creation that leaves our kitchen.

Our core values guide everything we do:

  • Love in every ingredient
  • Kindness in every interaction
  • Faith in the journey
  • Hope for growth and impact
  • Happiness in every treat
